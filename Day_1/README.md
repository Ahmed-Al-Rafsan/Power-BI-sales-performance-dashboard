**Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard**



This project showcases a \*\*sales performance dashboard built in Power BI\*\* using the Superstore dataset.  

The goal is to analyze \*\*sales, profit, and monthly trends\*\* to support business decision-making.



---



**Business Questions Answered**

\- Which product categories generate the highest sales?

\- Which categories are the most profitable?

\- How do sales trend month over month?



---



**Dashboard Overview**

> Note: This project was created using \*\*Power BI Desktop (Free Version)\*\*.  

> Due to publishing limitations, dashboard screenshots are provided instead of a live report.



**Sales \& Profit by Category**

\- Technology leads both \*\*sales and profit\*\*

\- Furniture shows lower profitability compared to sales

\- Office Supplies maintains stable performance



 **Monthly Sales Trend**

\- Overall upward sales trend

\- Noticeable growth in later months

\- Seasonal fluctuations visible across the year
